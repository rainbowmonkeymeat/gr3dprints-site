About GR3D Prints

Welcome to GR3D Prints! We're a small, family-run 3D printing business based in the greater Grand Rapids, Michigan area. It's just the two of us—me and my husband—working out of our garage with a couple of Bambu Lab X1C printers and a lot of creativity.

We've been 3D printing since 2010 and love bringing unique ideas to life, whether it's custom creations, replacement parts, or fun things for craft shows. This website is mainly a place for folks we meet at local events to reconnect, place custom orders, or grab something we might've sold out of at the show.

Thanks for stopping by—feel free to reach out if you have an idea you want to bring to life!
